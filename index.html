Encryption/Decryption Tool

Welcome to the Encryption/Decryption Tool. This tool uses a key to encrypt or decrypt your messages. It's crucial to use the same key for both processes.

Use the key to encrypt your message. To decrypt, you must enter the encrypted message and the correct key. If the key is incorrect, decryption will fail.

  1. Enter a Key: Choose a secret word or phrase. Use this key for both encrypting and decrypting messages.
  2. Type Your Text: Input the text you wish to encrypt or decrypt.
  3. Encrypt/Decrypt: Press 'Encrypt' to secure your message, or 'Decrypt' to revert to the original text. Ensure you use the same key used for encryption.
